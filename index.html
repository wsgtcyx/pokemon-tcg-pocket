How To Play Pokemon TCG Pocket

1. Build Your Deck

Create a balanced 60-card deck with:

  • Pokemon cards (20-25 cards)
  • Energy cards (15-20 cards)
  • Trainer cards (15-20 cards)

2. Learn Game Flow

Master the turn phases:

  • Draw a card
  • Attach one Energy card
  • Evolve Pokemon
  • Play Trainer cards
  • Attack with active Pokemon

3. Master Battles

Win by:

  • Defeating opponent's Pokemon
  • Collecting prize cards
  • Using type advantages

4. Grow Stronger

Expand your collection through:

  • Daily free booster packs
  • Battle rewards
  • Special events
  • Wonder Pick challenges

The Evolution of Pokemon TCG Pocket

1996: The Birth of Pokemon TCG

The Pokemon Trading Card Game debuted, laying the foundation for what would become Pokemon TCG Pocket.

1996-2023: Global Growth

Pokemon TCG expanded to 14 languages across 89 countries, inspiring the development of TCG Pocket.

2024: The TCG Pocket Revolution

Launch of Pokemon TCG Pocket, revolutionizing the traditional card game for the digital age.
